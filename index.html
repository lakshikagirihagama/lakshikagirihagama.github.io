I am currently working as a postdoctoral fellow at the Department of Physical and Enviromental Sciences, the University of Toronto. My supervisor is Professor Mathew Wells.

I did my PhD degree in Physical oceanography, Geophysical fluid dynamics at the Geophysical Fluid Dynamics Institute, the Florida State University, Tallahassee, Florida in 2015. I was supervised by Professor Doron Nof.

I did my B.Sc (Hons) Enviromental Engineering degree (major in Physical Oceanography) at the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka in 2009.

My research interests are Physical limnology, lake modeling, physical oceanography, climate, and fluid dynamics
